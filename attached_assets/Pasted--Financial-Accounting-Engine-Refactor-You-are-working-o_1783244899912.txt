# Financial Accounting Engine Refactor

You are working on an existing Medical Center Management System.

This is NOT a redesign.

Do NOT remove existing features.

Do NOT change the UI unless required.

Your task is to unify the accounting engine so every financial screen uses the same accounting rules.

---

# General Accounting Rules

The system must use the following accounting definitions everywhere.

## 1. Revenue

Revenue consists of ONLY:

* Patient payments
* Receipt Vouchers (Cash In)

Formula:

Revenue =
Patient Payments

* Receipt Vouchers

---

## 2. Expenses

Expenses consist of ONLY:

* Approved Purchase Requests
* Medical examination supplies and consumables
* Employee Personal Expense Payment Vouchers

Formula:

Expenses =
Purchase Requests

* Medical Supplies Cost
* Personal Expense Payment Vouchers

Do NOT include salaries here.

---

## 3. Salaries

Salary cost must be calculated separately.

Formula:

Employee Salary Paid

minus

(Employee Personal Expense Payment Vouchers)

minus

Approved Employee Advances

Net Salary Paid =
Salary
− Personal Expense Vouchers
− Approved Advances

This value is used as Salary Cost.

---

## 4. Patient Debts

Patient Debts are:

Money owed TO the medical center.

Only include unpaid patient balances.

Do not include insurance companies.

---

## 5. Insurance Company Debts

Insurance Company Debts are:

Money owed TO the medical center by insurance companies.

Only include unpaid insurance invoices.

---

## 6. Company Creditors

External Company Debts are:

Money the medical center owes TO suppliers or companies.

These are liabilities.

They must never be mixed with Patient Debts or Insurance Debts.

---

## 7. Profit

Profit must always be calculated as:

Profit =
Revenue
−
(
Expenses
+
Salary Cost
)

No other formula is allowed.

---

# Department Financial System

Each department must calculate independently.

Every department must have its own:

Revenue

Expenses

Profit

Patient Debts

Insurance Debts

Company Creditors

The department dashboard must never include values from another department.

---

# General Financial System (Administrator)

The administrator financial system must display totals for ALL departments combined.

The administrator pages must calculate:

Total Revenue

Total Expenses

Total Salaries

Total Profit

Total Patient Debts

Total Insurance Debts

Total Company Creditors

These totals must always equal the sum of all departments.

---

# Validation

Verify the following:

Every department calculates independently.

Administrator totals equal the sum of all departments.

Profit uses the same formula everywhere.

Salary is never counted twice.

Personal Expense Payment Vouchers reduce employee salary and are also recorded as department expenses.

Patient debts are separate from insurance debts.

Insurance debts are separate from company creditors.

No duplicated financial calculations remain.

Every report, chart and dashboard uses the same accounting engine.

Do not break existing APIs.

Do not remove existing functionality.

Preserve backward compatibility.
